Process Overview

A Step-by-Step Production Workflow

Our process is designed to reduce confusion and keep production aligned at every stage — whether you are launching a clothing brand or placing a corporate order. The workflow remains organized and transparent from start to finish.

Step 01

Share Your Requirements

Tell us what you want to produce. This helps us understand your exact production needs before any planning begins.

For Brands

  • Product type
  • Quantity
  • Fabric preference
  • Branding details

For Organizations

  • Apparel type
  • Total quantity
  • Size breakdown
  • Delivery timeline

Step 02

Production Planning

We review your requirements and prepare a structured production plan. You receive a clear overview before moving forward.

  • Fabric and material selection
  • Cost estimation
  • Production timeline

Step 03

Sampling & Development

Before bulk production, a sample is created. Adjustments are made at this stage if needed — this is your opportunity to get everything right.

Fabric Quality

Material confirmed and verified against specifications.

Fit & Sizing

Silhouette and measurements reviewed and verified.

Construction Details

Stitching method and build confirmed before bulk.

Branding Placement

Labels, trims, and branding elements finalized.

Happy with the sample? Production begins only after your approval. Start with a Sample

Step 04

Sample Approval

Production only begins after the sample is approved. This stage ensures the product matches your expectations, all details are confirmed, and the risk of production errors is significantly reduced.

  • The product matches your expectations
  • All details are confirmed in writing
  • Risk of production errors is reduced

Step 05

Bulk Production

Once approved, manufacturing begins in earnest. The entire production run follows the approved sample standard.

  • Production follows the approved sample
  • Quality checks carried out throughout
  • Progress monitored at each stage
  • Consistency maintained across all units

Step 06

Delivery & Ongoing Support

After production is completed, final products are prepared for delivery and dispatched. We maintain production records to make future orders faster and more consistent.

First Order

  • Final quality review
  • Prepared for dispatch
  • Delivered to specifications

Repeat Orders

  • Records kept from first run
  • Faster reorder timelines
  • Consistent garment quality
