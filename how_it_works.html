Step-By-Step Process

A structured, transparent production journey designed to keep every stage clear, controlled, and predictable.

Step 1

Share Your Requirements

Tell us what you want to produce.

For Brands:

  • Product Type
  • Quantity
  • Fabric Preference
  • Branding Details

For Organizations:

  • Order Type
  • Quantity Breakdown
  • Timeline
  • Customization Needs

"We review everything before moving forward."

Step 2

Review & Production Plan

We confirm:

  • Pricing Structure
  • Production Timeline
  • Quality Standards
  • Delivery Terms

Step 3

Sample Approval

For new designs or large orders, we prepare a sample for your approval.

You approve:

Fabric

Material confirmed for production.

Fit

Silhouette and measurements verified.

Construction

Stitching method and build approved.

Branding

Labels, trims, and identity finalized.

"The approved sample becomes the production standard."

Step 4

Production & Quality Checks

Production begins only after approval.

During Production:

Size breakdown tracked

Materials verified

Quality checkpoints applied

"No silent changes. No substitutions."

Step 5

Delivery

We coordinate delivery based on agreed timelines.

You receive:

  • Final production batch
  • Consistent quality
  • Clear documentation (for corporate orders)

Step 6

Reorder or Scale

Need more?

We maintain:

Fabric continuity

Same material source maintained.

Size breakdown records

Sizing ratios documented for repeat runs.

Branding setup

Labels, trims, and placements preserved.

"Scaling becomes easier because the groundwork is already done."
